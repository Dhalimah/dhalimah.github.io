Featured Research
Spatial Epidemiology · Public Health

ITN Malaria Coverage Analysis, Kano & Kaduna States

Comprehensive WHO/RBM 2024 indicator analysis of insecticide-treated net ownership, access, usage, and behavioural gaps across 67 LGAs in northern Nigeria. Spatial autocorrelation, hotspot detection, and bivariate choropleth mapping applied to ~161,000 individual and ~27,000 household records.

ArcGIS Pro Excel Moran's I Getis-Ord Gi* Bivariate Choropleth
Coverage 67 LGAs, 2 States
Records ~161,000 individuals
Framework WHO/RBM 2024 Indicators
Output Journal Article (in preparation)
Geology · Remote Sensing

Orogenic Gold Mineralization — Moro Area, Kwara State

Integrated ASTER multispectral imagery, Sentinel-1 SAR, aeromagnetic and aero-radiometric data to characterize hydrothermal alteration zones, structural frameworks associated with gold mineralization in the Nigerian schist belt and creating a Mineral Potential Map

ENVI PCI Geomatica ArcGIS Pro R Fuzzy AHP
Precision Agriculture · Soil Science

Soil Nutrient & Suitability Mapping — Ikere, Oyo State

Multi-season spatial analysis of 11 soil parameters (N, P, K, pH, Organic Carbon, and more) to produce an integrated suitability assessment for crop cultivation planning at IAR&T.

ArcGIS Pro QGIS Soil Classification Spatial Interpolation
Disaster Risk · Remote Sensing

Flood Extent & Vulnerability Mapping — Anambra West, Anambra State

Multi-temporal flood extent analysis using Sentinel-1 SAR (VH polarization) on Google Earth Engine for 2018, 2022, and 2024, capturing pre-flood and post-flood conditions for each year. Flood vulnerability zones were then mapped using AHP with nine conditioning factors: TWI, elevation, slope, drainage density, LULC, soil type, population density, distance to water bodies, and precipitation.

Google Earth Engine Sentinel-1 SAR ArcGIS Pro AHP SRTM DEM
Cartography · Topographic Mapping

Topographic Base Map — Idah-Igalamela, Kogi State

Production-grade topographic base map for Idah-Igalamela Local Government Area, Kogi State, commissioned by IAR&T. Includes full cartographic standards: contours, hydrography, settlements, and road networks.

ArcMap ArcGIS Pro DEM Analysis Cartographic Design
